As is evident from the graphs, there is a large prevalence of male characters in most Star Trek series. However, newer series tend to have a more equal female:male ratio, and the newest Star Trek series, Star Trek: Discovery, even has an almost 50/50 ratio. Also noticable is that no other character genders than female, and male appear in all series, for as far as they are known.
